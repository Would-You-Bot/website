## How can I make my Discord server active?

You might know the following scenario all too well: You've created a Discord server, but it's not very active. You're wondering how to make your Discord server active and what you can do to get more people in there. Well, we have some tips for you!

### 1. Make Your Members Feel Welcome

The first step to making your Discord server active is to make your members feel welcome. Many people would advice you to make a dedicated welcome channel, where new members are greeted with a message from a bot. While this seem like a good idea, it's not the best way to make your members feel welcome. Instead, you should greet the newly joined member directly in your general chat by yourself to give them a more warm welcome. This will make them feel more welcome and will make them more likely to stay in your server.

### 2. Be Active Yourself

We spoke to Howl, discord partner and owner of [Pottercord](https://discord.gg/pottercord), and TonyBytes, former discord partner and certified moderator, both of whom assured us that the number one piece of advice for a more active discord server is "[...] if the owner of the server is actively involved in their community. This is because people are more likely to stay on a server if they see that the owner is active and cares about the server. If you're not active on your server, people could think that you don't care about the discord server and will leave.

### 3. Use Engagement Bots

Engagement bots can add a lot of value to your server. They can help you keep your server active by providing fun activities for your members to do. They can also help you keep your server safe by providing moderation tools. There are many different types of engagement bots, and each one has its own unique features. 

- One of these discord engagement bots is [Would You](https://wouldyoubot.gg/invite), which happens to be our own bot. Would You is a discord bot that allows you to play would you rather, never have I ever, higher or lower, and what would you do on discord. It's a great way to break the ice and get people talking.

- Another great bot is [Tiny Games](https://top.gg/bot/878580419736506388), which offers a wide variety of fun and entertaining games for you to play with your friends on Discord, such as Rock Paper Scissors, Connect Rour and Tic Tac Toe. It's a great way to have fun with your friends and keep your server active.

### 4. Host Events

Hosting events can make your server more active by giving your members something to look forward to. Events can be anything from a movie night to a game night to a karaoke night. The possibilities are endless! Hosting events is a great way to get your members involved and keep them active on your server.

One event we used to do a lot was a 'finish my art' competition. It was held on a weekly basis and the community would vote on who finished the art the best. The winner would get a special role and a month of discord nitro or discord nitro basic. It was a great way to get people involved and keep them active on the server while giving back to the community.
