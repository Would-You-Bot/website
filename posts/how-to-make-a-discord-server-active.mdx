## How can I make my Discord server active?

You might know the following scenario all too well: You've created a Discord server, but it's not very active. You're wondering how to make your Discord server active and what you can do to get more people in there. Well, we have some tips for you!

### 1. Make Your Members Feel Welcome

The first step to making your Discord server active is to make your members feel welcome. Many people would advice you to make a dedicated welcome channel, where new members are greeted with a message from a bot. While this seem like a good idea, it's not the best way to make your members feel welcome. Instead, you should greet the newly joined member directly in your general chat by yourself to give them a more warm welcome. This will make them feel more welcome and will make them more likely to stay in your server.
